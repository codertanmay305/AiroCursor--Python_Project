AiroCursor - Your Virtual Mouse

Hand Gestures:

  • Move the cursor: Index finger tip
  • Right click: Index finger straight and middle finger bent
  • Left click: Middle finger straight and index finger bent
  • Double click: Closing all the fingers
  • Screenshot: 3 fingers up
  • Recording start: 4 fingers up
  • Recording stop: Fist
  • Scroll up: Thumbs up
  • Scroll down: Thumbs down
